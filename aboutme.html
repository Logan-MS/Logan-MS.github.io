About me: Logan Smiley


I have an associate's degree in Web Design and Development from Harrisburg Area Community College, and I've also completed several certifications from Linkedin Learning courses. Additionally, I received a certificate in UX design from Google through Coursera and am currently working on a certificate in Brand Identity from the London Business School on Coursera. You can view all of these certificates on my   LinkedIn page.


To reach me, you can email me at lsmileydesign82@outlook.com or on social media via Twitter messaging or LinkedIn. Phone number available on request.


I possess four years of experience working with HTML, CSS, and Photoshop, in addition to my education and certifications. I also have a fundamental understanding of working with WordPress.com, as well as Adobe Applications from their Creative Cloud suite, such as Adobe Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Audition, XD, Dreamweaver, Lightroom, After Effects, and Photoshop.


I have developed and honed various soft skills, including effective communication in person, over the phone, and in digital meetings. Over the years, I have gained valuable experience in problem-solving, from resolving issues with code to addressing miscommunications with clients and handling unforeseen incidents on the job. Additionally, I am an attentive listener who welcomes constructive feedback.


Aside from my education and professional experiences, I am fond of video games because they provide immersive experiences and thought-provoking narratives. I enjoy repairing and assembling projects as well as reading books. My favorite novel is Notes from the Underground by Fyodor Dostoevsky. I have a keen interest in the retrofuturism art movement and appreciate its creations, such as the Fallout game series. I have extensively studied history, and I enjoy discussing how we can learn from it to make the future better and help others.
