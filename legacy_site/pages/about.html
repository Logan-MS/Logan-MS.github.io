"I believe that people should take pride in what they do, even if it is scorned or misunderstood by the public at large."

Tony Hawk

Our founder, Matt Jeffery, is a life long skater. He wanted to share his passion with the world by making the best skating gear. After learning how to sew from his father, Matt started making his own skater clothes. Positive feedback from his friends led him to be inspired to share them with the world. Skate4Ever was founded on his passion of making high quality skater wear which later branched out into decks and other board parts. Currently, Matt enjoyes skating at his local Washington skate park.
