Logan Smiley Web Designer


Hi, my name is Logan Smiley. I graduated from HACC with an associate's degree in web design and development. I have always had a passion for the internet and technology. Web design allows me to be part of what I have loved for years creating websites that satisfy my client's needs to help their brand shine. It truly is a rewarding experience.


One of my favorite designs is the concept website shared on this page, which I had the pleasure of designing with some colleagues.


I have had four years of HTML and CSS experience. After being encouraged by my high school web design teacher, I decided to follow this career path. I have experience with Adobe Creative Cloud applications such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe Dreamweaver, Adobe Audition, and Adobe Premiere Pro. In addition, I follow all the current web design trends by reading blog posts by design and technology sites such as Design Shack, Lifewire, Pantone, PC Mag, CNET, and Adobe design articles.


I specialize in working with HTML and CSS with some basic Javascript knowledge. I also have extensive skills in UX design and earned a certificate from Google's course on Coursera. I possess familiarity with creating prototypes, style guides, and web compositions. In addition, I am savvy with graphic design and photography. I have studied typography for the web, Bootstrap 4. Managing social media accounts and creating content for accounts is another mastery in my repertoire, running a blog and Instagram account for a specific interest garnering up to 500 followers over a year.
