Logan Smiley Web Designer


Hi, my name is Logan Smiley. I am currently studying at HACC for web design and development. I have always had a passion for the internet and technology. Web design allows me to be part of what I have loved for years creating websites that satisfy my clients' needs to help their brand shine. It truly is a rewarding experience.


I designed the concept website to the right. It is one of my favorite designs.


I have had 3 years of HTML and CSS experience. After being encouraged by my high school web design teacher, I decided to follow this career path. I have an understanding of most Adobe Creative Cloud applications including but not limited to, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe Dreamweaver, Adobe Audition, Adobe Premiere Pro. In addition, I follow all the current web design trends by reading blog posts by design and technology sites such as Design Shack, Lifewire, Pantone, PC Mag, CNET, and Adobe design articles.


I specialize in working with HTML and CSS with some basic Javascript knowledge. I have experience with creating prototypes, styleguides, and web compositions. In addition, I have experience with some graphic design and photography. I have studied typography for the web, Bootstrap 4, and have a beginner knowledge of Javascript frameworks such as React.
